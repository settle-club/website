About us

At Settle, we are revolutionizing the way businesses offer credit and financing options to their customers. As a leading multi-lender credit enablement platform, we empower brands, enterprises, and financial institutions with innovative, customizable, and scalable lending solutions.Our Mission Our mission is to democratize access to credit by providing seamless, technology-driven financing options that cater to every sector and customer type. We aim to enhance customer experiences, boost sales, and drive growth for businesses of all sizes.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Lending Solutions: From Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) to personal loans, invoice financing, supply chain financing, and more, our platform supports a wide range of credit products tailored to meet your business needs.
  • ‍Seamless Integration: Our no-code platform comes with ready-to-use SDKs and extensions for popular e-commerce platforms like Shopify, Magento, WooCommerce, and more, ensuring a quick and effortless integration.
  • ‍White-Labeling: Customize our platform with your brand's identity and offer financial services under your own name. Our white-label solution is pre-integrated with top lenders, giving you the flexibility to expand your financial offerings.
  • Modular Approach: Leverage specific modules like our Business Rule Engine (BRE), Underwriting Engine, or KYC Module, all designed to integrate seamlessly into your existing systems.

Why Choose Settle?

  • Tailored to Your Needs: Whether you're an enterprise looking to offer financial services or a platform seeking to enhance your checkout experience, Settle provides solutions that are fully customizable and scalable.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Access deep analytics and insights through our robust merchant and customer management tools, helping you make informed decisions that drive growth.
  • Global Reach: With a presence across multiple markets, we provide wide area coverage and higher approval rates through our multi-lender system.
