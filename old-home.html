Cardless EMI

Cardless EMI allows individuals to buy and pay in installments without a credit card, often partnering with financial institutions for direct installment plans.

Accessibility
Flexibility
Convenience
How it works
  1. When making a purchase, individuals can choose the option to pay using cardless EMI at participating merchants or online stores.
  2. Customers can complete a straightforward online or in-store application, providing basic information like name, contact details, and proof of identity/address.
  3. The financial institution evaluates eligibility by considering factors like creditworthiness and repayment history. Upon approval, the customer receives confirmation of their EMI plan.

What is Settle?

Settle is the new BNPL platform that offers instant credit with just a few simple steps. Settle limit offers instant credit up to ₹ 20,000 is a 100% paperless process.

All you need is an Aadhaar card and a PAN card to open a Settle account.Enter your Aadhaar linked phone number, complete the KYC and your Settle account is created.

Does Settle charge any application fee?

No! Creating a Settle account is 100% free, with no application fee or any hidden charges.
